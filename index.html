The Team

Meet Team Exceed

Scott Jarrett

Licensed Salesperson · REAA 2008

Scott is a seasoned real estate agent with over 25 years of experience in the Bay of Plenty, known for connecting clients with excellent homes through a genuine and positive experience.

He effectively utilizes Arizto's advanced sales techniques, offering digital platforms, complimentary marketing, and competitive commissions.

Beyond real estate, Scott has served as a Whakatāne District Councillor for 12 years and is recognised for his involvement in Theatre Whakatāne. His deep understanding of the region's history and infrastructure enhances his ability to serve clients effectively.

Stuart Lougher

Licensed Salesperson · REAA 2008

Stuart's passion for sales began in the motor trade before transitioning to real estate in 1992, a field in which he has thrived for 32 years.

Now with Arizto, his success is reflected in a strong track record of repeat and referral business, underscoring his dedication to exceptional service in real estate transactions.

Stuart is committed to his community, having served as Chairman of the Whakatane Community Patrol for a decade and was instrumental in setting up a CCTV network to enhance safety in key town areas. Always approachable, Stuart remains available to assist with queries and provide guidance.

Michelle Gibbons-King

Licensed Salesperson · REAA 2008

Michelle has been part of the real estate world for over 20 years, working across roles including office manager, salesperson, and personal assistant.

This wide-ranging experience means she really understands how the industry works and how to support clients through every step of their property journey.

Now as a Sales Associate and PA with Team EXCEED, Michelle continues to bring her knowledge, organisation skills, and friendly approach to help deliver great results.
